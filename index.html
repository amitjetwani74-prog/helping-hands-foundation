Our Story

Making a Difference
Since 2010

Helping Hands Foundation was born from a simple belief — that every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and care. Founded in 2010 by a group of dedicated social workers in Jaipur, we have grown into a nationally recognized NGO serving millions across 28 states.

Our Vision

A just, equitable, and compassionate India where no one is left behind.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities through sustainable development programs.

Integrity Compassion Transparency Inclusivity Sustainability
